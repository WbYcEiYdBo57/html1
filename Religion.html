Light Pantheon

The Light Pantheon represents all that is good in the world. It is led by the Goddess of Light, Love, Fertility, Joy and Mercy, Alathia.

Dark Pantheon

The Dark Pantheon represents all that is dark and corrupt in the world. It is led by the God of Madness, Ancients, and Dark Knowledge, Hastur.

World Pantheon

The World Pantheon represents nature itself and everyhting that resides in it. It is led by the God of the Wild and Nature, Val.

Astral Pantheon

The Astral Pantheon represents the Astral Plane and all things otherworldly. It is led by The Void Empress.

Mortal Pantheon

The Mortal Pantheon represents civilization. It is led by The God of Justice, Law and Order, Gideon.

Hell Pantheon

The Hell Pantheon represents the Nine Layers of Hell and the Devils that reside in it. It is led by the King of Hell Zero.

Heaven Pantheon

The Heaven Pantheon represents Neutrality, Balance and the Afterlife. It is led by the God of Knowledge, Time and Fate, Karma.

Warrior Pantheon

The Warrior Pantheon represents all those that walk the path of a warrior. It is led by the God of Primal Power, Ignis.

Magic Pantheon

The Magic Pantheon represents everything that has to do with magic and all those that use it. It is led by the God of Magic, Loafus Cramwell.

Hidden Pantheon

The Hidden Pantheon represents trickery, deception and those that hide in the shadows. It is led by the Goddess of Vampires, Deceit, and Seduction, Lilith.
